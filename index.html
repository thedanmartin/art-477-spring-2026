Art 477 Spring 2026 Anthology

Animated Book Advertisements

Using HTML and CSS, students create a dynamic advertisement for a book of their choice. Two animations must play upon opening the webpage and a third is revealed when the user moves their mouse over the book cover.

  • Itzayana Aguilar, The King in Yellow
  • Andrew Jakes, Metamorphosis
  • Sophia Lefeber, Sunrise on the Reaping
  • Iva Miletic, At Midnight Comes the Cry
  • Grey Miller
  • Jack Miller, Jujutsu Kaisen Volume 26
  • Austin Moser
  • Elizabeth Nehmzow, Earthlings
  • Alexandra Rothwell, The Rainbow Fish
  • Camaren Wick, TRON Legacy
  • Aaron Zachar, The Creative Act: A Way of Being
